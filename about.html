Cybersecurity, Computers, & Cats Edward Ayala

          I am an education focused technology enthusiast who loves anything and everything to do with computers . I have a deep passion for Cybersecurity, my love for computer science stemmed from a bumpy beginning in glitching and breaking software and webapps as a young teen. I endeavored to learn more by teaching myself more exploit techniques via dodgy forums and YouTube videos.

          Once obtaining my Associate's degree from Tarrant County College , I continued my education at Texas Wesleyan University with the intention of earning a bachelor's degree in Computer Science. Two years later I enhanced my programming skills and understanding of networks , software development , and algorithms to become confident with all sects of Computer Science.

          I enjoy teaching others about Computer Science, especially Cybersecurity. My main outlet being a mentor for high school CyberPatriot teams, and an instructional assistant at Tarrant County College. I believe that teaching others about security basics can help create a smarter online population, less suseptable to malicous agents.
